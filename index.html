Privacy Policy for Sapien.Network

Effective Date: 20 November 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sapien.Network! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when using our application in conjunction with the TikTok API.

2. Information We Collect

Sapien.Network does not collect or store any personal information. Our use of the TikTok API is solely to post videos on the account that you authorize. No user data is collected, shared, or stored.

3. Use of TikTok API

We use the TikTok API only to manage and post videos to your TikTok account, as authorized by you. The app does not request or access any data other than what is necessary for posting videos.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there are no additional security measures required. Authorization tokens provided by TikTok are securely managed and used exclusively for their intended purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not share any data with third-party services. The TikTok API is used only to interact with TikTok accounts.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at sapien.network2024@gmail.com.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
